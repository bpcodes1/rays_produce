SECTION 2 — SEO FRAMEWORK GAMEPLAN
================================================================================

GOAL: Rank for local and industry-specific searches so Ray's Produce
appears when restaurant owners, retailers, and food service managers
search for produce distributors in Oregon and Washington.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2A — TARGET KEYWORDS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  PRIMARY (brand / direct searches):
    - "Ray's Produce"
    - "Rays Produce Salem Oregon"
    - "Ray's Produce Inc Salem OR"

  HIGH-INTENT LOCAL (what customers search before finding a distributor):
    - "produce distributor Salem Oregon"
    - "restaurant produce supplier Oregon"
    - "food distributor Salem OR"
    - "wholesale produce Oregon"
    - "local produce delivery Oregon"
    - "produce distributor Portland Oregon"
    - "restaurant food supplier Pacific Northwest"
    - "wholesale food distributor Oregon Washington"

  LONG-TAIL (easier to rank, high conversion):
    - "daily produce delivery Salem Oregon restaurants"
    - "local wholesale produce supplier Willamette Valley"
    - "fresh produce distributor for restaurants Oregon"
    - "family owned produce distributor Oregon"
    - "produce delivery service Eugene Oregon"
    - "restaurant supplier Portland Oregon"
    - "food service distributor Pacific Northwest"

  SERVICE AREA PAGES (one per region = more ranking opportunities):
    - "produce distributor Portland Metro Oregon"
    - "produce delivery Eugene Oregon"
    - "restaurant supplier Salem Oregon"
    - "wholesale produce Eastern Washington"
    - "food distributor Puget Sound Washington"


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2B — ON-PAGE SEO (per page)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Each page needs:
    - Unique <title> tag (60 chars max)
    - Unique <meta name="description"> (150–160 chars)
    - One H1 per page (includes primary keyword)
    - H2s for major sections (secondary keywords)
    - Alt text on all images (descriptive + keyword)
    - Internal links between pages

  Planned title tags:
    /           → "Ray's Produce | Fresh Produce Distributor Salem, Oregon"
    /about      → "About Ray's Produce | Family-Owned Since 1996 | Salem, OR"
    /services   → "Our Services | Wholesale Produce & Food Distribution | Ray's Produce"
    /contact    → "Get a Quote | Ray's Produce Distributor Salem, Oregon"

  Planned meta descriptions:
    /           → "Ray's Produce Inc. is a family-owned produce distributor serving
                   restaurants, retailers, and wholesalers across Oregon and Washington
                   since 1996. Daily deliveries. Competitive pricing."
    /about      → "Learn the story behind Ray's Produce — a family-owned food distributor
                   in Salem, Oregon dedicated to quality, community, and lasting partnerships
                   since 1996."
    /services   → "From fresh produce to bakery products, Ray's Produce offers daily
                   food distribution for restaurants, retailers, wholesalers, and processors
                   across the Pacific Northwest."
    /contact    → "Ready to partner with Ray's Produce? Request a custom quote today.
                   Serving 50+ cities across Oregon and Washington with daily deliveries."

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2D — TECHNICAL SEO
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Site structure:
    - Clean URLs: /about, /services, /service-areas, /contact
    - No trailing slashes inconsistency
    - 301 redirects if any old URLs exist

  Performance (Core Web Vitals — Google ranking factor):
    - Compress all images (WebP format)
    - Lazy load images below the fold
    - Minimize render-blocking CSS/JS (Vite handles this well)
    - Target: Lighthouse score 90+ on mobile

  Mobile-first:
    - All pages fully responsive (already being built this way)
    - Touch targets 44px minimum
    - Font size 16px minimum on mobile

  Files to create:
    - /public/robots.txt
        Content: "User-agent: * / Allow: /"
        Add sitemap URL: "Sitemap: https://raysproduceinc.com/sitemap.xml"
    - /public/sitemap.xml
        List all 4 page URLs with <lastmod> dates
    - /public/favicon.ico + /public/apple-touch-icon.png
        CLIENT TO PROVIDE: final logo for favicon

  HTTPS:
    - Ensure SSL certificate is active on hosting (required for SEO)
    - All URLs serve over https://


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2E — STRUCTURED DATA / SCHEMA MARKUP
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  What it does: Tells Google exactly what the business is — can unlock
  rich results (star ratings, hours, address) in search results.

  Schema types to implement:

  1. LocalBusiness (on every page via <head>)
     {
       "@type": "LocalBusiness",
       "name": "Ray's Produce Inc.",
       "description": "Family-owned produce distributor serving Oregon and
                        Washington restaurants, retailers, and wholesalers.",
       "foundingDate": "1996",
       "address": {
         "@type": "PostalAddress",
         "addressLocality": "Salem",
         "addressRegion": "OR",
         "addressCountry": "US"
       },
       "areaServed": ["Oregon", "Washington"],
       "telephone": "CLIENT TO PROVIDE",
       "url": "https://raysproduceinc.com"
     }

  2. Organization (homepage)
     - Name, logo, URL, social profiles, founding date

  3. BreadcrumbList (About, Services, Contact pages)
     - Helps Google understand page hierarchy
     - Shows breadcrumb trail in search results

  4. FAQPage (Contact page FAQ section)
     - Each Q&A pair marked up
     - Can appear as expandable FAQ directly in Google search results

  5. Service (Services page)
     - Mark up each service offering individually


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2F — OPEN GRAPH & SOCIAL META TAGS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  What it does: Controls how pages look when shared on Facebook,
  LinkedIn, iMessage, etc. Also used by some search tools.

  Add to <head> on every page:
    <meta property="og:type"        content="website" />
    <meta property="og:site_name"   content="Ray's Produce Inc." />
    <meta property="og:title"       content="[page title]" />
    <meta property="og:description" content="[page description]" />
    <meta property="og:url"         content="[full page URL]" />
    <meta property="og:image"       content="/og-image.jpg" />
    <meta name="twitter:card"       content="summary_large_image" />

  OG Image:
    - 1200x630px image (CLIENT TO PROVIDE or create from brand assets)
    - Shows when someone shares the website link
    - Should include logo + tagline
